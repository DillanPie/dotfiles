# 📁 Dillan Suon's Portfolio - Cybersecurity & Programming

Welcome to my personal portfolio website! This site showcases my skills, projects, and resume. It also features a clean, dark-themed design inspired by the **Gruvbox** color palette.  

## 🌟 Features
- 💻 **Responsive Design:** Looks great on desktop and mobile devices.
- 🖼️ **Image Slideshow:** Auto-playing slideshow with smooth transitions and pause-on-hover functionality.
- 📂 **Project Showcase:** Links to various personal projects with descriptions.
- 📑 **Downloadable Resume:** Easy access to my resume in PDF format.
- 📬 **Contact Form:** Simple form for reaching out to me directly.

---

## 📸 Slideshow Enhancements
- Automatically generated navigation dots.
- Smooth transitions between slides.
- Pauses on hover for better control.
- Keyboard navigation support.

---

## 📝 Technologies Used
- **HTML5 & CSS3:** For structure and styling.
- **JavaScript:** For interactive slideshow and navigation.
- **Gruvbox Theme:** Consistent color palette throughout the website.
- **Nerd Fonts:** For visually appealing icons and design.
